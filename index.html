Welcome to the "Acting with Chatbot" experience, which will help you to develop your acting skills. The scene in this acting practice comes from the "Pride and Prejudice" film (2005), where Elizabeth rejects Mr. Darcy's proposal. You are Elizabeth and chatbot is Mr. Darcy. To begin, press the start button and read the first line in the script. Speak clearly and confidently. If chatbot doesn't reply at any time, repeat the last sentence in your lines.
